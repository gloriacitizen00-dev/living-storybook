# 📖 Living Storybook

Welcome to Living Storybook, an original manga and interactive storytelling project created in Costa Rica by Gloria Martinez.

The story follows two siblings on an extraordinary journey alongside their friends as they discover their true powers and face increasingly powerful enemies. Throughout their adventure, new allies, mysterious creatures, and formidable villains are introduced, revealing a world where courage, friendship, and hope become the greatest strengths.

At its heart, Living Storybook explores the timeless idea that light can overcome darkness, even in the most challenging circumstances.

Rather than presenting a traditional digital comic, this project transforms storytelling into an interactive web experience by combining illustration, narrative design, and modern web technologies.

---

# 🌟 About the Project

Living Storybook is an original intellectual property that combines manga-inspired storytelling, digital illustration, front-end development, and interactive design into a single creative experience.

Every aspect of the project has been conceived and developed by the author—from the original story, characters, world-building, and visual style to the website interface and user experience.

The project serves both as a creative portfolio and as an exploration of how traditional storytelling can evolve into immersive digital experiences while preserving the emotional connection between readers and illustrated narratives.
